#Urban Economics, Land Markets, and Housing Supply
Research on urban land markets, housing supply, land values, rents, development timing, and the interaction between regulation, infrastructure, and market outcomes. I am especially interested in empirical and theoretical work that explains how subdivision, rezoning, infrastructure capacity, landowner behaviour, and planning uncertainty shape housing delivery and land price dynamics. Papers are most relevant when they distinguish demand-side pressure from supply-side constraint, and when they help inform planning reform, development feasibility, land release strategy, or land value capture in real urban contexts.

#Spatial Equilibrium, Housing Allocation, and Urban Simulation
Research on spatial equilibrium modelling, residential sorting, gravity models, housing allocation, land use simulation, and integrated urban-economic modelling. I am especially interested in methods that link households, employment, accessibility, housing supply, land values, and regulatory or infrastructure constraints across space. Papers are most relevant when they offer implementable modelling approaches, calibration strategies, or empirical applications that can support scenario planning and policy analysis, especially in fragmented or supply-constrained urban systems.

#Property Markets, Development Feasibility, and Urban Forecasting
Research on property market dynamics, development feasibility, absorption, construction cost, cap rates, rental performance, and urban forecasting. I am especially interested in work that helps explain when and where development occurs, how viability is shaped by regulation and infrastructure, and how market conditions affect urban growth trajectories. Papers are most relevant when they support practical forecasting, scenario testing, or feasibility analysis rather than purely financial engineering.

#Land Value Capture, Infrastructure Finance, and Municipal Coordination
Research on land value capture, development charges, betterment levies, infrastructure finance, cost recovery, fiscal incidence, and the role of municipal coordination in unlocking development. I am particularly interested in work that examines how infrastructure provision, regulatory change, and coordinated public action create land uplift, and how municipalities can recover or structure part of that value efficiently and equitably. Papers are most relevant where they engage with implementation and governance challenges in growing or capacity-constrained urban settings.

#Evaluation of Public Safety and Policing Technologies
Research on the effectiveness, efficiency, and broader impacts of policing and public safety technologies, including CCTV, acoustic gunshot detection, drones, aerial surveillance, and related monitoring systems. I am particularly interested in rigorous causal evaluation using quasi-experimental methods, spatial analysis, event studies, synthetic control, treatment intensity approaches, and related designs. Papers are most relevant where they examine crime outcomes, operational efficiency, resource deployment, displacement, spillovers, implementation conditions, or cost effectiveness. I am less interested in purely technical surveillance studies without credible evaluation of outcomes.

#Urban Building Detection, Settlement Mapping, and Applied Geospatial Measurement
Research on the use of remote sensing, building footprint data, population surfaces, satellite imagery, aerial imagery, and geospatial analysis to identify buildings, map settlement extent, measure urban expansion, and derive practical urban indicators. I am especially interested in work that supports urban and regional planning, growth modelling, infrastructure assessment, or population allocation. Papers are most relevant when they focus on buildings, settlements, urban land use change, or applied geospatial measurement for the built environment. I am less interested in papers focused primarily on vegetation, atmospheric conditions, environmental monitoring, or general Earth observation methods without a clear urban built-environment application.

#Applied Urban and Regional Growth Modelling
Research on land use growth modelling, spatial development scenarios, regional forecasting, infrastructure-led growth, and applied modelling approaches used in planning and policy. I am especially interested in models that support municipal growth management, nodal planning, infrastructure sequencing, and the translation of spatial data into implementable decision tools. Papers are most relevant where they bridge academic rigour and practical planning application, rather than abstract mathematical modelling of urban form with limited policy relevance.

#Cost-Benefit Analysis, Infrastructure Economics, and Energy Reliability
Research on cost-benefit analysis, project appraisal, infrastructure economics, energy reliability, peaking generation, electricity supply security, and the valuation of public or strategic infrastructure. I am particularly interested in methods for assessing reliability, resilience, system value, uncertainty, and wider economic effects in cases such as open cycle gas turbines and other infrastructure with high strategic importance. Papers are most relevant where they support applied appraisal and decision-making rather than purely technical engineering optimisation.

#Freight Logistics, Road-to-Rail Transition, and Perishable Value Chains
Research on freight transport, logistics systems, modal shift from road to rail, corridor efficiency, terminal systems, and the economics of moving fresh produce and other time-sensitive goods. I am especially interested in work on generalised cost, reliability, cold chain requirements, intermodal systems, and the institutional barriers to more efficient freight systems. Papers are most relevant when they can inform corridor planning, feasibility assessment, or comparative logistics strategy.

#Archaeology and Historical Geography of Sicily
Research on the archaeology, historical geography, and long-term settlement history of Sicily. I am especially interested in work on Greek colonisation, indigenous societies, Roman Sicily, late antiquity, Arab-Norman Sicily, military history, and the relationship between landscape, routes, ports, and political change. Papers are most relevant when they help interpret specific places, sites, routes, or regions in Sicily, and when they are analytically grounded rather than purely descriptive.

#Ancient Sicily and the Central Mediterranean
Research on ancient Sicily in its wider central Mediterranean context, including interaction with southern Italy, Carthage, the Greek world, and Rome. I am particularly interested in connectivity, trade, colonisation, conflict, sanctuaries, urbanism, and regional power shifts. Papers are most relevant where they illuminate Sicily’s role within broader Mediterranean systems and use archaeological, historical, epigraphic, or spatial evidence in a rigorous way.
